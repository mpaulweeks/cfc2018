It's like the natural selection that all life went though to be what it is, except guided by the animal that essentially won evolution. And considering that even the top machines have only had maybe 100 years to get to where they are now—compared to us humans who've had hundreds of thousands of years, I'm very curious and excited to see what AI will be able to do in my lifetime.
